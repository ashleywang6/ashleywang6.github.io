Hello again! I'm Ashley.

I'm a problem solver who loves challenges. I've always been interested in design, technology, innovation, and leadership in school. It all started when me and my team proposed an app concept tailoring checking drug interactions at the 2018 BC Pharmacy Association Student poster competition. After graduation, I discovered and fell in love with the field of HCI. I love UX design because it embodies many skills of a healthcare professional. For instance, practicing empathy, utilizing user-centric approach, and understanding the needs of others.

Outside of work, I love to volunteer and read books focusing on personal development and psychology. These experiences has changed how I perceive myself and others around me. Having a growth mindset truly helped me understand the "WHY" behind people's words and actions. With a growth mindset, I continuously push myself outside of my comfort zone and explore the impossible possibilities. I want to make people's life easier and happier by designing modern and effortless products that addresses their needs.
